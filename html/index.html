App Description

Users

We will cater towards college and university music creatives of all types. However also music creators and enthusiasts will be a secondary base.

Date & Information

The information users will be viewing are aimed at facilitating promoting and collaboration among college and university music creatives. This means users can see a musician's list of skills, the genres of songs they produce, the school they are currently attending, the number of followers they have, who they are following, and links to other external apps such as YouTube, Instagram, or Spotify. Furthermore, users can also see music snippets that musician uploaded on to their account page. Each snippet will show a title, an author, a cover image, a description, the length of the snippet, and the number of likes and comments the snippet has. Users can also see posts made by a musican, and the comments and likes on that post.
