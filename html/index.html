Interaction / Solution:

The app is designed to be simple and straightforward, because we think that the app should work intuitively,
so we used some common design and features that are seen in other social apps. The app will enable users to
search based on their interests, for example, if a UW student looks for a singer near their location, they
can just search based on school. It totally makes sense because if they want to form a group, location matters
sometimes. They could search based on skill and genre too; many singers have different tastes and skill. The app
encourages users to share their snippets online because they want to share their songs and if someone likes their
songs, they could have the opportunity to interact with other musicians; users can leave comments on each other.
The app helps college students to be creative and innovative.

The site will also encourage engagement with other users through interactive posts and an appealing interface.
The app aims to be easy to navigate and personalized for ones customizable profile and experience, using elements
and design choices based on existing social media and networking platforms such as Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn.
Because the app is made for musicians and publicizes music as a whole, we want users to have a sense of familiarity
when using the platforms features such as chatting with other musicians, uploading files relating to music, and
playing uploaded snippets of music (which is also inspired by Spotify). We will prioritize user profiles as the key
to forming networks with other users and presenting information that helps create impressions, through displaying
skill sets, an “About” section, and uploaded Snippets to gauge their works and skill level.
